Children's Fairyland
Brand: Oaklandish
This design is an homage to an Oakland jewel: Children's Fairyland! The storybook theme park has delighted children and parents alike since 1950, and continues to be a magical world where young children can create, imagine, play, and learn. 10% of proceeds from this design will be donated to Children's Fairyland so that the park can enjoy many more years of happily ever after.
