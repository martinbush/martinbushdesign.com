JOGGER SWEAT SUIT
PROJECT:
Create a staple silhouette for Oaklandish private label.
Simple, fuctional, technical.
In early 2017 we experimented with screen printing on windbreakers we had sourced. The response was very positive so we took the next step and developed our own windbreakers overseas.
FEATURES:
SCUBA NECK
ELASTIC DRAW CORDS IN HEM AND HOOD
DWR (DURABLE WATER REPELLENT) COATING
ZIPPERED FRONT POCKETS
YKK ZIPPERS
3-PANEL HOOD
UNDERARM VENT EYELETS
RETRO NAUTICAL SLEEVE PRINT
100% NYLON
