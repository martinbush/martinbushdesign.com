Sunset Bear

CONCEPT - A FRESH TAKE ON THE ICONIC CALIFORNIA BEAR

Imagery inspired by Japanese Woodblock prints and local Bay Area print artist Tom Killion. Marrying the iconic California bear silhouette with a scene reminiscent of the North San Francisco Bay spanning a full range of color.

Done in two styles: Bust and Full Bear both with multiple Colorways for Men and Women.
