Success Animation
When a customer finishes making a custom product, we want them to feel excited. We want to celebrate that moment with them while ushering the user from the cart back to the homescreen. While animations can be delightful, they can also be discracting and misleading. Our goal was to confirm the finshed product, highlight the cart icon for checkout, and promt the user to continue shopping.
