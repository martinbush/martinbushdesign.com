Champion X Oaklandish
Screen Printing, Embroidery
Winter 2018
Jumbo Script Reverse Weave Sweatsuit
A contemporary Streetwear x Collegiate aethestic for Oaklanders. Champion recently exploded onto the streetwear scene and we wanted to seize the chance to collaborate with iconic hoody maker. Collegiate athletic apparel offers a lot when it comes to pride of place. This collection offers up an aethetic somewhere between hypothetical UC Oaklandish gear and contemporary streetwear fleece.
