Overview
Re-designing the photobook customizing UI
Skills and Tools
FramerJS, Coffee Script, Illustrator, Photoshop
Designing the Cover
The first step for making a beautiful photobook, designing the cover. User is asked to upload a photo and choose from a selection of cover templates.
Filling your book
User is asked to upload 20-76 photos fill thier photobook. User can place each photo on a page or use the autofill feature.
Page Layout Options
Each page can be formated to fit 1-2 photos. Full bleed or with borders. User can even spread one photo across two pages.
Tips and Caption options
Help text appears on hover for buttons and fuctions. User can add captions to the caption friendly templates.
Reordering Photos and Pages
Drag and drop to reorder photos and page spreads.
